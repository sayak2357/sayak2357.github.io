    Introduction

    There are 3 main NLP conferences namely ACL, NAACL and EMNLP. Each year papers are published pertaining to different categories as long, short, research workshops, demonstration etc. Here we have processed the meta-data regarding the Short and Long papers.

    The meta-data contains author names and year of publication. The extra meta-data generated by the OCR++ application are affiliation, author-emails,footnotes and url. The different plots are made here by permuting the mentioned meta-dataset.
